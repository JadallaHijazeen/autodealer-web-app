About Us

Since 2010, AutoDealer Co. has been committed to delivering top-notch vehicles and unparalleled customer service. Over the past decade, we’ve grown our fleet to over 500 vehicles, ranging from fuel-efficient compacts to luxury SUVs and electric models. We carefully maintain each car to ensure your ride is always comfortable, reliable, and safe.

Our mission is simple: make renting a car as hassle-free as possible. Whether you’re traveling for business, exploring Jordan on a road trip, or simply need a convenient ride around Amman, our easy online booking system and transparent pricing keep stress at bay. We believe in honest service—no hidden fees, no surprises.

  • Extensive Fleet Options: Economy, Compact, Sedan, SUV, Luxury, EV
  • Flexible Rental Plans: Hourly, Daily, Weekly, Monthly—and special weekend packages
  • 24/7 Roadside Assistance: Peace of mind from the moment you grab the keys
  • Loyalty Rewards: Earn points on every booking, redeemable for free rental days

We’re proud to be a locally-owned Jordanian company with a global mindset—partnering with major hotels and travel agencies to bring you seamless pick-up/drop-off options. Our friendly customer support team is always just a call or email away. Book today and discover why thousands trust CarRental Co. for their journeys.
